### Customizable Parameters for Your AI Agent:

1. Configure Reward System:
   - Distribution Method: Choose between targeted wallet distribution or reward all token holders.
   - Distribution Frequency: How often your AI agent distributes rewards. (Options: Hourly, Daily, Weekly, Monthly)

2. Customize Profit Sharing:
   - Creator Fee: Percentage of profits retained by you, the AI agent creator. (Range: 0-100%)
   - Airdrop Frequency: How often your AI agent distributes profits to token holders. (Options: Hourly, Daily, Weekly, Monthly)

3. Risk Management:
   - Risk Tolerance: Determines your AI agent's willingness to take on risk. (Scale: 1-10, 1 being very conservative, 10 being very aggressive)
   - Maximum Daily Loss: The maximum percentage your AI agent is allowed to lose in a single day. (Range: 1-20%)
   - Leverage Use: The maximum leverage your AI agent can use. (Range: 1x-5x)

4. Yield Farming:
   - Minimum APY Target: The lowest annual percentage yield your AI agent will consider for farming. (Range: 1-100%)
   - Farm Duration: The preferred length of time for your AI agent's yield farming positions. (Options: Short-term (1-7 days), Medium-term (1-3 months), Long-term (3+ months))
   - Compounding Frequency: How often your AI agent reinvests yields. (Options: Hourly, Daily, Weekly)

5. Liquidity Provision:
   - Pool Size Preference: The size of liquidity pools your AI agent prefers. (Options: Small (<$100k TVL), Medium ($100k-$1M TVL), Large (>$1M TVL))
   - Rebalancing Frequency: How often your AI agent adjusts its liquidity positions. (Options: Hourly, Daily, Weekly)

6. Token Selection:
   - Market Cap Focus: The range of token market capitalizations your AI agent considers. (Options: Top 10, Top 50, Top 100, Top 500)
   - Stablecoin Allocation: Percentage of the portfolio your AI agent allocates to stablecoins. (Range: 0-100%)

7. Trading Style:
   - Trading Frequency: How often your AI agent makes trades. (Options: Low (1-3/day), Medium (4-10/day), High (11+/day))
   - Strategy Split: Balance between trend following and contrarian strategies for your AI agent. (Range: 0-100% Trend Following, with the remainder being Contrarian)

8. Advanced Features:
   - Use of Yield Aggregators: Whether your AI agent uses yield aggregators. (Options: Yes/No)
   - Cross-Chain Bridges: Whether your AI agent utilizes cross-chain bridges. (Options: Yes/No)
   - Options/Derivatives Trading: Whether your AI agent engages in options or derivatives trading. (Options: Yes/No)

9. Social Trading (X/Twitter):
   - Sentiment Analysis Weight: How much your AI agent considers social media sentiment in its decisions. (Range: 0-100%)
   - Influencer Signal Consideration: Whether your AI agent takes into account signals from influential accounts. (Options: Yes/No)
   - Trending Token Weight: How much emphasis your AI agent places on trending tokens. (Options: Low, Medium, High)

10. Performance:
    - Target Annual Return: The yearly return your AI agent aims to achieve. (No set minimum or maximum; you can input your desired target)
